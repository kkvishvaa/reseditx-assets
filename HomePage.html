About ResEditX

ResEditX is a Google Docs add-on that uses artificial intelligence to help you create ATS-friendly resumes and optimize your content for better job application success.

With ResEditX, you can extract job keywords, analyze ATS compatibility, and get AI-powered suggestions to improve your resume — all inside Google Docs.

Key Features

  • AI Keyword Extraction: Pulls keywords from job descriptions automatically.
  • ATS Score Analysis: Get compatibility scores and recommendations.
  • Bullet Point Optimizer: Transform and generate impactful resume bullet points.
  • Smart Text Processing: Optimize selected text or enter your own for AI suggestions.
  • Privacy First: No data storage — everything stays in Google Docs.
